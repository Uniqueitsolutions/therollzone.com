Who we are

ABOUT ROLL ZONE

Founded in 1998, we specialize in crafting precision custom rollers for the paper, printing, steel, and textile industries. Our expertise lies in manufacturing high-quality rubber-coated rollers and offering re-rubberizing services, alongside a range of repair and maintenance solutions
We have a strong track record of collaborating with renowned paper and printing companies and extending our reach to international markets, including Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Kenya, and other emerging regions.
Our dedicated team of technical professionals is committed to delivering exceptional solutions to our valued partners.

Extra Info About Paper Industry

ROLLZONE utilizes advanced rubber material technology to optimize the performance of various production line sections, including forming, pressing, drying, and finishing. Our roll covers incorporate state-of-the-art polymers and cutting-edge technologies, ensuring exceptional wear resistance, stable roughness, hardness, and durability even in challenging operational conditions, resulting in uninterrupted roll performance.

Extra Info About Roll Covering

Roll covering is an industrial product used to coat cylinders that are parts of textile machines. These cylinders are involved in the production and transformation of natural and synthetic fibers, such as in the spinning, weaving, and dyeing processes.
