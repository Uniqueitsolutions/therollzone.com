Thickness 10 – 20mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 110° C

ASH-TOUCH

Hardness 85 - 96° A
Thickness 15 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 105° C
This new engineered rubber cover is suitable for heavy nip loads at elevated temperature on MG. It has got excellent wear and heat resistance. The rubber is tough enough at heavy loads.

ASH-ROCK

Hardness 0 – 1 P&J
Thickness 11 – 20mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 90° C
This is a superb hard cover for Top Press, Size Press position and has good wear and tear resistance property. The cover can withstand doctoring also.

ASH-TOUCH HR

Hardness 90 - 92° S A
Thickness 15 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 140° C
This is a specialty rubber coated roll for MG Touch Roll. It features excellent heat resistance property, good elasticity and good chemical and wear resistance.

ASH-STONE C

Hardness 0 – 1 P&J
Thickness 11 – 20mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 120° C
This is a rock hard cover with outstanding abrasion resistance. This cover fulfills the sheet release property and enjoys good repellence. This can be easily doctored and enjoys resistance to press part chemicals. This cover is usually used in Centre Press rolls and Top Press Rolls.

Hardness 30-45° SA
Thickness 15-35 mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 80°C
Very soft, non adhesive rubber cover for Couch Rolls and Lump Breaker Rolls. It has got excellent wear and chemical resistance property. It breaks the lumps to form uniform pulp.

ASH-PRESS

Thickness 15 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 100° C

ASH-TAN

Thickness 7 - 15mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 80°C

ASH-TECH PRESS

Hardness 82 - 95° A
Thickness 15 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 110° C
Highly versatile rubber cover for heavily nip loaded Press Rolls and Blind Drilled Press Rolls. This cover has got excellent heat characteristics and high wear and tear resistance.

ASH-TECH HEAVY PRESS

Hardness 88 -92° S A
Thickness 15 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 120°C
This cover is especially engineered for heavily nip loaded (25-400kg/cm) Press Rolls. This cover enjoys anti hysterics property. It has got highly abrasion resistance and wear and tear resistant property.

ASH SOFTEX LB

Hardness 35 - 45° SA
Thickness 15 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 80° C
It is a soft elastomeric compound that Lump breaking rolls need to continuously release fiber from their surface. It's low but uniform pressure smoothens and dewaters the paper as it leaves the fabric, providing the desired finish. It is resistant to most paper making chemicals. Most suitable for Lump Breaker Rolls application.

ASH-KOTE

Hardness 65 -75° SA
Thickness 20 – 25mm
Temperature Resistance Up to 120°C

ASH-CONVEY

Thickness 15-20mm
