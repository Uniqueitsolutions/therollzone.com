ABOUT ROLL ZONE

Founded in 1998, we specialize in crafting precision custom rollers for the paper, printing, steel, and textile industries. Our expertise lies in manufacturing high-quality rubber-coated rollers and offering re-rubberizing services, alongside a range of repair and maintenance solutions
We have a strong track record of collaborating with renowned paper and printing companies and extending our reach to international markets, including Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Kenya, and other emerging regions.
Our dedicated team of technical professionals is committed to delivering exceptional solutions to our valued partners.
