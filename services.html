Our

Services

We provide complete solution of rubber rollers including new rolls, refurbishing, repairing and grinding of rollers for Paper Manufacturing, Printing and Packaging, Steel and Textile industries.

New Rolls:

ROLEZONE is manufacturing new rolls for all types of Industries i.e. Paper, Textile, Steel, Printing etc. we manufacture the following rolls:
  • TOP PRESS ROLLS
  • BOTTOM PRESS ROLLS
  • COUCH ROLL
  • BREAST ROLL
  • POWER PRESS ROLL
  • FELT ROLLS
  • IDLER ROLLS
  • CONVEYOR ROLLS
  • SHEET CUTTER ROLLS.
  • MANDRELS and rolls of many other types for other allied industries.

Recoating

ROLEZONE is doing recoating for all types of industries, (list given under the INDUSTRIES / INDUSTRIES SERVICED). We are using all types of elastomers which are used for various applications. ROLEZONE is also doing coating of SILICONE rubber which can be used for extreme conditions.

Job Works:

ROLEZONE is doing job works on all types of rolls e.g. ies
  • Repair of bearing sizes.
  • Replacement of complete shafts
  • Dynamic Balancing
  • Lining of Tanks
  • Dynamic balancing of all rolls

Grinding

ROLEZONE is having 4 big grinding machines (please see list of machinery). We are doing GRINDING AND CAMBERING with accuracy on all types of Press and Calendar Rolls. One machine is dedicated to the Parallel grinding of the felt and other straight rolls. Our GRINDING machine capacity and our CRANE Capacity enables us to grind some of the biggest rolls in the industries.

Dynamic Balancing:

ROLEZONE is capable of doing Dynamic Balancing for all types of rotating bodies. Weghing upto 50 MT. The regular products that we do Balancing are...
  • Dryers
  • Press Rolls
  • ID Fans
  • FD Fans
  • Pulpers Impellers
  • Turbine Rotors
